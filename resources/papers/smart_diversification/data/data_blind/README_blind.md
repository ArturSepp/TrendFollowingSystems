# Blinded replication data

The two files in this folder are the allocator input sheet and the monthly
excess-return panel of the paper, with overlays anonymised under the
manuscript labels CTA 1-5, LS 1-5, and QIS 1-5. Every exhibit of the fund
and allocation sections reproduces from these files alone. The mapping to
the underlying products is withheld for double-blind review and is
available from the authors on acceptance.
